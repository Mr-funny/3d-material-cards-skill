# Series art direction

Generate a single 2:3 **card-front artwork**, not a physical card mockup. Use an ink-dark midnight navy base, antique-gold engraved borders and cloud/star motifs, jade lacquer and aged bronze accents, plus one restrained vermilion/rust accent. Make the figure sharply readable and central. Require clear differences between matte paper, polished gilt, lacquer, fabric, and armor so material maps have meaningful data.

Always request: crisp line engraving, embossed-looking ornamental border, no text/numerals/logos/watermark, no rainbow foil, no blur, no duplicate figure, no cropped weapon, no outer background beyond the card face, and unlettered decorative cartouches. Give each character a distinct signature action; avoid repeating a static frontal standing pose.

## Character prompt variables

| Character | Required identity details | Signature action |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Zhao Yun | silver-white armor, wind-and-cloud heraldry, spear, composed youthful commander | alert three-quarter guard with spear held across the body |
| Zhang Fei | dark armor, leopard-head spear, powerful bearded commander, black and deep plum accents | low braced stance with spear planted or raised defensively |
| Ma Chao | pale silver armor, white cloak, spear, western frontier motifs and cold mountains | advancing diagonal spear sweep or thrust, body twisting in motion |
| Huang Zhong | seasoned elder archer, bow, bronze armor, autumnal gold and subdued crimson accents | full bow draw with arrow aimed across the frame |

Maintain the same border density, dark paper ground, gold linework and restrained lighting across the set. Change only character identity, supporting motifs, and accent colors.
